Progression Rules
Clean all sets before adding weight.
Upper body increases in smaller jumps.
Lower body can progress faster, especially early.
When equipment tops out, add reps or slow tempo.
Do not force progression during shoot-heavy weeks.
Travel Rules
Use bodyweight upper/lower sessions to maintain tension.
Keep protein high and meal timing steady.
Do not let one bad travel day become a bad week.
Camera-Ready Rules

Weeks 9–12 are the lock-in phase.
Alcohol early is limited; late phase is none.
Zevia is fine; sugary cheat drift is not.
